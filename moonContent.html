Moon Facts

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite.

It is about 1/4 the diameter of Earth.

The Moon takes 27.3 days to orbit Earth.

It always shows the same face to Earth.

Moonlight is actually reflected sunlight.

It influences ocean tides on Earth.

There are many craters formed by impacts.

A lunar day is about 29.5 Earth days.

It has been visited by astronauts 6 times.

The far side of the Moon is not always dark.
